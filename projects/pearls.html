// Selection

Two-Stage Selection

1 Momentum Ranking

All stocks sorted by momentum score. Top N stocks (configurable per market) pass to the second stage. This ensures we only consider stocks with strong, consistent upward trends.

Sort by Score Top N Selection Min Momentum Filter

2 Pearl Score

Multi-criteria quality assessment. Evaluates inverse volatility (price/ATR), volume consistency, and short-term momentum confirmation.

Inverse Volatility Volume 90d/30d Momentum 30d

3 Gap Filter

Final filter removes stocks with large price gaps that could indicate news-driven moves rather than sustainable trends.

Gap Detection Outlier Removal Quality Control
